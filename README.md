How to Play
Kaboo is a 2-player card game played with a standard 52-card deck.
Each player starts with a 2×2 grid of face-down cards. The remaining cards form a draw pile, and a discard pile is created during play.
Objective:
Finish the game with the lowest total score in your 2×2 grid.
Turn Actions:
On their turn, a player must choose one of the following:
1. Draw from the deck
o If it’s a normal card: swap it with one of your cards or discard it. o If it’s a power card: either use its effect or swap/discard it.
2. Draw from the discard pile
o Swap the card with one of your grid cards.
3. Knock
o End the round. All other players get one final turn before scoring.
Power Cards:
 7 / 8 → Peek at one of your own cards.
 9 / 10 → Peek at an opponent’s card.
 Jack → Swap one of your cards blindly with an opponent’s card.
 Queen → Peek at one of your cards and one opponent card, then optionally swap them.
Scoring:
 Ace = 1 point
 Number cards = face value
 Jack / Queen = 10 points
 King = –1 point
The game ends when a player knocks or the deck runs out. The player with the lowest score wins. Tech Stack / Implementation
 Kotlin, Gradle, BGW, JUnit5
 Hotseat mode + GUI logic
